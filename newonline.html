Important Information

01/

WINTER TRAINING SESSIONS

If you are taking part in evening training sessions please be aware that lighting around walkways etc might be limited. Come prepared

02/

MEDICAL ARMBANDS

Medical armbands must be warn at all training sessions. If you are unable to comply with this then please prepare an emergency contact sheet and hand to your trainer prior to the session

03/

CANCELLATION

Entries via online system If the session is cancelled, entrants will be informed by phone, text or email. Most sessions require a minimum of 3 people to run. If you need to cancel your session please contact the club. Unless we can fill the spot no refunds can be given.

04/

DRESS CODE

There are no formal requirements for turnout and tack for training sessions but we do insist that riding hats and suitable footwear are worn at all times.

05/

UNABLE TO ENTER ONLINE?

If you are unable to enter online the please contact the club on training@huntingdonrc.co.uk and we will do our best to arrange an alternative method of booking in.

06/

FEEDBACK

We are always keen to get your feedback on sessions and also to hear what other training sessions or trainers you would like to see available. Do get in touch.

07/

IMPORTANT

Make sure you are ready to ride at the allocated start time Most importantly - enjoy your session!

Tara has produced a number of young horses from backing through their competitive career. Tara uses a sympathetic structured method of training for both horse and rider and trains each combination individually to suit their requirements. Long Drove Dressage - Tara

Training at Houghton Hall

We hold regular training at Houghton Hall on Mondays - morning and evening sessions. Indoor and outdoor sessions. These are very popular and fill up quickly. During evening winter sessions the walkways and car park can be very dark. Please come prepared.

Training at Home Farm

Home Farm Alconbury is a lovely friendly venue with arena, grass riding areas and a super cross country course. Loads of parking at the bottom fields and space at the top arena too
