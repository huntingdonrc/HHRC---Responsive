LINCOLN CHAMPIONSHIPS

The Seniors had a very wet day of competition at the Lincoln Summer Champs but still had a great day with some super results. The Juniors had a much better day with the Weather and also did themselves proud.

ARENA ETIQUETTE.

Just a polite reminder. When visiting a venue for an event or training arranged by the Club, please remember to pick up your horse's droppings, especially from inside the arena. Spectators too are requested to help their team mates, and the Club, by ensuring droppings are collected. It is very important that you do this, as not doing so reflects badly on the whole Club and could lead to the Club being banned from using the venue in future. Thank you.

TRAINING

TRAINING AT LOCAL VENUES

The majority of our sessions are now available for online entry. This is our preferred method of entry as it keeps the administration to a minimum for our team of volunteers. In the event of cancellation of any session any person that has booked in will be notified by text, phone or email. If you have any questions on training then please email the club huntingdonrc@gmail.com. If you are not able to book in online then please download the training booking form below and send a cheque which must arrive at least 2 days prior to the training event. Do let us know if there is a particular trainer or discipline you would like us to use
