Showjumping Teams

We are very pleased to have Sam Morgan as our new Show Jumping Team Chef.  Sam has been involved with the club for a long time and has always been a highly active member of all of our teams.  Sam will be organising some SJ training ahead of the Qualifiers so if you are wanting to compete, thinking of competing or just want to have some fun with SJ then do get in touch with Sam and let her know.  Sam can be contacted on sj@huntingdonrc.co.uk

Winter Show Jumping Qualifier - Sunday 31st January 2016 - Houghton Hall Equestrian Centre

A super day was had by all. We had some great results in both juniors and seniors with the Junior Novice team qualifying for the Championships. A huge thanks to Sam, Sarah and Becca for organising and supporting everyone. Thanks also to our team helpers who gave up their time to cover our helper slots. The teams are very grateful - we could not have done it without you.

Summer SJ Qualifier - Sunday 19th July 2015

The Show Jumping qualifiers were hosted by Cambridge & District Riding club and held at Earith.  We had Junior, Senior and Grassroots teams competing in Show Jumping and Style Jumping.  The weather was kind to us and the event was well attended by members – either riding or helping.  We had 34 entries from our club – the most of any of the clubs. 

  • Junior Novice team (Ellie, Phoebe, Emily & Georgie) came 2nd with an individual 5th for Phoebe.  The Junior team scooped 1st in Style Jumping and qualified for Lincoln.

  • Senior Intermediate saw an individual 2nd for Sam Morgan

  • Senior Novice Clubs (Sarah Grout, Katie Grout,  Penny Hipwell, Alison Brock) were unplaced but Sarah achieved an individual 4th.

  • Senior Novice Spades (Alison Jones, Sarah Anderson, Sam Morgan, Olivia Mejias) came 6th with an individual 2nd for Sarah

  • Senior Style Diamonds (Sam, Alison, Sarah A, Katie L) claimed 2nd with an individual 2nd for Katie, 3rd for Sam and 5th for Alison.  Our other style team, Hearts (Karen Neal, Sam, Rebecca, Penny) just missed out on being placed

  • Senior Open team claimed 1st and qualified for Lincoln.  Well done to Sam Morgan, Katie Larman and Nikki Goldup

  • Grassroots team also claimed 1st.  Congratulations to Karen Neal, Sam Morgan, Cathryn Pratt and Sarah Anderson

SJ Intermediate Qualifiers - Milton College 6th April 2015

Easter Monday dawned bright and sunny for the first running of the intermediate SJ qualifier at the College of West Anglia. Houghton and Huntingdon had one senior team entered for the competition, comprised of Katherine Peden riding Rufus, Danny Harrison riding Wallace, Rebecca Robinson riding Trequite and Nikki Wallace riding Wayward Wanderer. The course was tight and twisty with the first round at 90cm which the team flew meaning we finished the first round on a score of 0! A tougher course of 100cm followed which caused more issues. Unfortunately our first 3 riders each had an unlucky 4 faults, but a fabulous clear from Nikki meant that the team finished on 8 faults gaining a fabulous 3rd place! Everyone had a great day in the sunshine and we had team spirit in abundance if any other members would like to take part in our next summer qualifiers please e-mail me on kas214@hotmail.co.uk. The Winter Intermediate Team Show Jumping qualifier will be held on Monday 6th April (Easter Monday) at the College of West Anglia, Milton, Cambridge, CB24 6DB.

The Intermediate Winter Championships will take place on 2nd, 3rd May at Bury Farm Equestrian Village, Slapton, Bucks LU7 9BT
